# Leaderboard and Rankings

The SONAR leaderboard shows the top creators ranked by total points. It drives competition, recognition, and community engagement.

## How Rankings Work

**Primary Metric**: Total accumulated points (all-time)
**Secondary Metrics**: Tier, number of submissions, average rarity score
**Update Frequency**: Hourly
**Historical Snapshots**: Daily (for tracking trends)

Your rank is determined by comparing your total points to all other creators on the platform.

## Viewing the Leaderboard

**Global Leaderboard**: Top 100 creators worldwide, ranked by points

**Filter Options**:
- By Tier: View only Bronze, Silver, Gold creators
- By Time Period: Last 30 days, last 90 days, all-time
- By Category: Subject expertise (birds, speech, equipment, etc.)
- Search: Find specific creators by username or wallet

**Information Shown**:
- Rank (1-100+)
- Username or wallet address
- Total points
- Tier badge (Contributor, Bronze, Silver, etc.)
- Number of submissions
- Average rarity score
- First bulk contributions count
- Rare subject contributions
- Last submission date

## Tier-Based Leaderboards

**Contributor League**: Ranks 1-100+ starting at 0 points
**Bronze League**: Ranks 1-50+ at Bronze tier or higher
**Silver League**: Ranks 1-30+ at Silver tier or higher
**Gold League**: Ranks 1-20+ at Gold tier or higher
**Platinum+ League**: Top 15 Platinum and above

Each league shows relative ranking within that tier group.

## Leaderboard Updates

### Hourly Updates

Each hour:
- Rankings recalculate based on latest points
- New submissions are scored
- Positions shift as creators earn points
- Notifications sent if you gain/lose positions

### Daily Snapshots

Every 24 hours:
- Snapshot of your position
- Historical record preserved
- Trend analysis available
- Archive of historical ranks

## Leaderboard Dynamics

**Moving Up the Leaderboard**:
- High-quality submissions move you up
- Each submission adds points
- Rank updates hourly
- Consistent uploads maintain momentum

**Staying at Top**:
- Requires continuous submissions
- Target rare subjects (higher points per submission)
- Bulk submissions gain positions quickly
- Consistency matters over time

**Falling Down**:
- If others earn points faster, you drop
- Your points never decrease
- But relative rank might decrease
- Competition is constant

## Leaderboard Strategies

**To Climb Quickly**:
1. Focus on high-rarity subjects (5.0x multiplier)
2. Bulk submit (2.0x bonus for first bulk)
3. High quality (1.5x multiplier)
4. Be specific (1.3x multiplier)
5. Submit consistently (more points per time = faster climb)

**To Stay at Top**:
1. Regular submissions (every 1-2 weeks)
2. Maintain quality standards
3. Explore new rare subjects (saturation grows)
4. Engage with community (builds reputation)

**To Maximize Longevity**:
1. Diversify subjects (avoid saturation in one area)
2. Build sustainable catalog
3. Target underexplored areas
4. Long-term strategy over quick gains

## Leaderboard Integrity

**Anti-Gaming Measures**:
- Duplicate submissions detected and rejected
- Spam audio filtered during verification
- Saturation penalties prevent oversaturation
- Manual review of suspicious activity

**Fair Competition**:
- Points are awarded objectively
- No favoritism or manipulation
- Blockchain records all transactions
- Community can audit leaderboard
